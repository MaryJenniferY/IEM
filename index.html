About

An outstanding educational family in the industry- focussed scholastic training consortium of today, the IEM group has set paramount standards in coping up with the technical and managerial shortcomings in the new age of active globalism. The IEM group rose to fame because of its robust base in training & R&D in complex zones. Its main focus is to attend to the new age group and the country with its pledge to autonomy and exceptional superiority.

With its 2 UGC recognized universities (UEM Kolkata and UEM Jaipur) and one AICTE approved and UGC recognized institute (IEM Kolkata) in India, engaged in imparting UG, PG, and Ph.D. courses, it is spearheading the challenges posed by the current industrial trends.

IEM Kolkata was established in 1989 and has been ranked as 191st in the latest NIRF ranking for engineering institutes across India. UEM Kolkata was established in 2014 by Act No 25 of Govt. of West Bengal, was awarded the "AAA" category in the NPTEL program. UEM Jaipur was established in 2011 by Ordinance 11 of 2011 and Act No 5 of 2012 of Govt. of Rajasthan was awarded as the "Best Private University in Rajasthan" in the National Education Excellence Awards, 2016.
